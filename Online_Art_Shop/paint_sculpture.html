PAINTING & SCULPTURE



Art comes in various forms. The average person judges art primarily based on appearances. However, there is so much more that sets one art form apart from another. Painting and sculpting are two of the most well-known art forms. They are both rich in history and continue to have a tremendous effect on the art scene of the current decade. Despite having similar origins and historic progressions, some fundamental differences make painting and sculpting different from one another.
